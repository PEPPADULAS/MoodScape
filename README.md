# 🌸 MoodScape - Seasonal Thoughts Journal

A beautiful, mood-based journaling platform that combines the art of self-reflection with immersive seasonal themes and music integration. Capture your thoughts, track your emotions, and create soundscapes that match your inner world.

![MoodScape Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400/4F46E5/FFFFFF?text=MoodScape+-+Your+Seasonal+Journal)

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 **Dynamic Seasonal Themes**
- **5 Beautiful Themes**: Spring 🌸, Summer ☀️, Fall 🍂, Winter ❄️, Rainy 🌧️
- **Auto Theme Detection**: Automatically adapts to current season
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Seamless switching with system preference support
- **Custom Gradients**: Beautiful gradient backgrounds that match each season

### 📝 **Smart Journaling**
- **Mood Tracking**: Tag thoughts with emotions and weather conditions
- **Rich Text Editor**: Intuitive writing experience
- **Smart Tags**: AI-powered tag suggestions
- **Private/Public Posts**: Control visibility of your thoughts
- **Search & Filter**: Find thoughts by mood, season, or keywords

### 📊 **Analytics & Insights**
- **Mood Timeline**: Visual representation of emotional patterns
- **Activity Heatmap**: Track your journaling habits
- **Word Cloud**: Discover your most used words and themes
- **Seasonal Analytics**: Compare thoughts across different seasons
- **Growth Tracking**: Monitor your emotional journey over time

### 🎵 **Music Integration**
- **Playlist Management**: Create seasonal playlists
- **Music Visualizer**: Beautiful audio visualizations
- **Queue System**: Manage your listening experience
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Quick music controls
- **Mood-Based Recommendations**: Music that matches your emotions

### 🏆 **Gamification & Achievements**
- **Writing Streaks**: Maintain daily journaling habits
- **Seasonal Badges**: Unlock achievements for each season
- **Progress Tracking**: Celebrate milestones and growth
- **Achievement Celebrations**: Beautiful animations for accomplishments

### 📱 **Modern Experience**
- **Responsive Design**: Perfect on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Gesture Support**: Intuitive touch interactions
- **Accessibility**: Full keyboard navigation and screen reader support
- **Progressive Web App**: Install as a native app
- **Performance Optimized**: Fast loading and smooth animations

## 🚀 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org/) with React 19
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com/) with custom seasonal themes
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) for smooth interactions
- **Database**: [Prisma](https://prisma.io/) with SQLite (easily configurable for PostgreSQL)
- **Authentication**: [NextAuth.js](https://next-auth.js.org/) with multiple providers
- **UI Components**: [Radix UI](https://radix-ui.com/) primitives
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Build Tool**: Turbopack for fast development
- **TypeScript**: Full type safety throughout the application
